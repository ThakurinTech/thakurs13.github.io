Digital Studies

The University of Chicago’s program in Digital Studies of Language, Culture, and History provides a one-year Master of Arts curriculum intended for full-time students who have a bachelor’s degree in the humanities or in a related discipline such as history, anthropology, or linguistics.

RSTI

The Ras Shamra Tablet Inventory presents a catalog of inscribed objects from Ras Shamra-Ugarit. The catalog is based on and updates Bordreuil and Pardee TEO.
